About kivu belt

Discover the crest of adventure with a visit to the Kivu Belt and escape into one of the diverse tourism offers along the lake. Start your action packed holiday with a hiking or biking tour. Visit cultural tourism projects or simply relax & enjoy the stunning views over the lake. With its lush green hills, magnificent and endless scenery, majestic volcanoes, evergreen rain forests, wildlife and biodiversity, and above all the warmth of the Rwandan people, the beauty of Rwanda does not end, and neither does ADVENTURE
